27/12/2019

Posted on 2019-12-27 | In lifestyle

It’s a normal Friday, 12 days before we could fly back home. I feel i’m just waiting to get back to China for the new year since I got nothing serious to do here. Christmas and the Boxing day had passed but this year was disappointing cause nothing we need or we could buy were on sale. My girlfriend bought me a perfume for my coming birthday and some lipsticks for her families, that’s all we got on the Boxing Day.

We have planed for years to travel to Europe and finally it’s happening in the next Feburary. Schengen applied and flight booked, but we are not going along, we go as a two couple group with my girlfriend’s twin sister and the boyfriend (which might become my sister and brother in law, geez!). My girlfriend made much effort to make that happen, felt like we were all being dragged together by her. well as long as she’s happen, I could cope with that, for once. We are going to spend two weeks in four coutries, I know that sounds weird, but we really don’t have enough time and there are so many places I’d like to see. Afterall this won’t by my last visit to Europe, I’m sure I would have more chance to see the beatiful continent in the future.

I bought the DJI’s Osmo Action (just like GoPro but cheaper) and mavic mini cause I want to shoot some pictures while we are travelling. I have never been a good photographer but I hope I would improve. I hope the local police would not sent me back for flying a drone above the Colosseo or something like that. Anyway, I’m looking forward to this journey, hope for the best. Cheers.
